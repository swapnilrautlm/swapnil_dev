How each study day runs

Math block · ~2–3 hrs
  1. Khan (learn): open the day's unit & lesson — read the "| Lesson", watch the Top-tips + Worked/Quick-example videos, then do the leveled practice ("get 3 of 4 to level up").
  2. Question Bank (drill): ~10–12 questions on the same skill at the day's difficulty.
  3. Review + log every miss to the Google Sheet.
English block · ~2–3 hrs
  1. Khan (learn): the day's R&W lesson — "| Lesson" + "Top tips" + Worked example, then the leveled practice.
  2. Question Bank (drill): a set on the same skill.
  3. Wren & Martin (reinforce): read the mapped chapter + a few exercises (grammar days).
  4. Review + log.
