How each study day runs now

English — the main event · ~2.5–3.5 hrs
  1. Grammar & Expression block (first): Khan U10/U12 grammar or expression drill + the mapped Wren & Martin chapter → Question Bank set. This is where the recoverable points are.
  2. Reading block: Khan reading lesson (Advanced/Challenge) → Question Bank set (hard) — hold and extend the gains.
  3. Full timed R&W module in the pre-test slots.
Math — maintenance · ~1–1.5 hrs
  1. One short hard set on the rotating domain (mostly Data & Geometry; Advanced Math occasionally) — verify in Desmos, watch grid-in precision.
  2. Full timed Math module only before tests, to hold pacing.
  3. Re-work the latest test's misses.
